How to Play

Overview

Spelling Mastery tests your mastery of academic vocabulary used in HUMSS subjects. A definition or clue is shown ‚Äî choose the correctly spelled word from three options before the timer runs out!

Difficulty Levels
  • Easy: 20 foundational HUMSS terms and general knowledge (e.g., Equality, Psychology, History)
  • Normal: 20 intermediate vocabulary including philosophy, social science, and history (e.g., Epistemology, Onomatopoeia, Hypocrisy)
  • Hard: 20 advanced academic terms requiring deeper knowledge (e.g., Bureaucracy, Denouement, Conscientious)
Gameplay Steps
  • 1. Choose your difficulty and press Start Game
  • 2. Read the definition or clue carefully
  • 3. Click the option with the correct spelling (A, B, or C)
  • 4. Answer before the timer hits zero!
  • 5. Use hints wisely to eliminate a wrong choice
